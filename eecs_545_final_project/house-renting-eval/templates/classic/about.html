About HomeNest Rentals

Our History

HomeNest Rentals was founded in 2011 by lifelong friends Marcus Webb and Diana Okafor, both of whom had firsthand experience navigating the frustrations of renting in a new city. After years of dealing with unresponsive landlords, misleading listings, and surprise fees, they set out to build something different: a rental platform built on transparency, trust, and tenant respect.

Starting with just 8 listings in Austin, Texas, HomeNest grew steadily through word of mouth. By 2015, we had expanded into three additional states and partnered with over 200 independent property owners who share our commitment to honest, well-maintained housing. Today, HomeNest manages over 1,400 active listings across 12 major US cities, and our team of 60 staff members is dedicated to matching renters with homes they are proud to live in.

Our mission has never changed: make renting straightforward, fair, and human. We vet every property before it goes live, enforce clear lease standards, and respond to every inquiry within one business day. Whether you are a first-time renter or relocating across the country, HomeNest is here to make the process feel manageable.
