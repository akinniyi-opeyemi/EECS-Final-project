📖 Biography

Microeconomic theory and the design of economic institutions.

Gerhardt Vössing is Professor of Economics at Halvern University, where he has taught since 1991. His research is in microeconomic theory, with particular emphasis on mechanism design, auction theory, and the foundations of general equilibrium. Before Halvern, he held positions at the University of Bonn and was a visiting member of the Institute for Advanced Study. He received his Ph.D. from Princeton University in 1987 under the supervision of the late Hugo Sonnenschein. He has served as co-editor of Econometrica (2002-2007) and as an associate editor of the Journal of Economic Theory and Games and Economic Behavior. He is a Fellow of the Econometric Society.
