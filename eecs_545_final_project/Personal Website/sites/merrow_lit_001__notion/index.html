👤 About

Yuki Abernathy is an Assistant Professor in the Program in Literature, Science, and Technology at Merrow Institute of Technology. Their research sits at the intersection of twentieth-century American fiction and the history of computation, with a particular interest in how mid-century writers imagined — and argued with — the machines being built around them. Their first book project, Compiled Fictions, traces the entanglement of postwar American novelists with early programming cultures at Bell Labs, RAND, and the Stanford AI Lab. Before joining Merrow in 2023, they completed a Ph.D. in English at Duke University and a postdoctoral fellowship in the Digital Humanities Initiative at the University of Virginia.
